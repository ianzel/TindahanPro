# **TindahanPro – Sari-Sari Store Management System**

*(Small Business Finance & Inventory Focus)*

---

## 1. Project Overview

TindahanPro is a web-based Sari-Sari Store Management System designed to help small store owners efficiently manage daily business operations. The system focuses on financial tracking, inventory monitoring, sales recording, and customer credit management.

It provides a simple yet powerful digital solution for traditional neighborhood stores by organizing transactions, computing profits, tracking stock levels, and generating useful reports for better decision-making.

The system emphasizes practicality, real-world usability, and business-oriented financial management.

---

## 2. Project Objectives

* To digitize sari-sari store record-keeping
* To automate profit computation and stock deduction
* To improve inventory monitoring and restocking decisions
* To track daily sales performance
* To manage customer credit (“utang”) efficiently
* To apply financial computation and business logic in a web application

---

## 3. Target Users

* Small sari-sari store owners
* Micro entrepreneurs
* Family-run businesses
* Community retail shop operators

---

## 4. Scope of the System

The system manages:

* Product inventory
* Daily sales transactions
* Profit tracking
* Supplier information
* Customer credit records
* Business performance reports

It is intended for single-store usage and operates as a standalone web application.

---

# 5. Core Features (Finance & Inventory Foundation)

---

## 5.1 Product Management

The system allows the store owner to manage product information.

Features include:

* Add new products
* Edit existing product details
* Delete products
* Categorize products (e.g., beverages, canned goods, snacks, toiletries)
* Set buying price and selling price
* Track available stock quantity

The system maintains updated inventory records for all products.

---

## 5.2 Sales Recording

The system allows the user to record daily sales transactions.

Features include:

* Select product sold
* Enter quantity purchased
* Automatically calculate total sale amount
* Automatically deduct sold quantity from inventory
* Store transaction date and time
* Save completed sale records

The system ensures that stock levels are updated in real time after each sale.

---

## 5.3 Daily Sales Summary Dashboard

The dashboard provides a quick overview of business performance.

Displayed information includes:

* Total Sales Today
* Total Profit Today
* Number of Transactions
* Low Stock Items
* Total Inventory Value

This enables the store owner to immediately assess daily performance.

---

# 6. Unique Features (Store-Specific Enhancements)

---

## 6.1 Inventory Alert System

The system monitors stock levels and automatically identifies low-stock items.

Features:

* Highlight products below minimum stock level
* Display “Restock Needed” label
* Show alert notification on dashboard
* Optional filter to view only low-stock items

This helps prevent out-of-stock situations and lost sales.

---

## 6.2 Profit Calculator & Financial Reports

The system automatically computes profit per product and per transaction.

It generates:

* Daily profit reports
* Weekly profit summaries
* Monthly profit summaries
* Overall revenue and expense comparison

This helps the store owner understand income trends and financial health.

---

## 6.3 Supplier Management

The system keeps records of product suppliers.

Features include:

* Add supplier details (name, contact number)
* Assign products to suppliers
* View supplier list
* Track which products come from which supplier

This simplifies restocking and supplier communication.

---

## 6.4 Best-Selling Products Analytics

The system analyzes sales data to identify top-performing products.

Features include:

* Top 5 best-selling products
* Sales ranking by quantity
* Sales trends chart
* Filter by date range (daily, weekly, monthly)

This supports better stocking decisions and business strategy.

---

## 6.5 Credit (“Utang”) Tracking System

This feature allows the store owner to manage customer credit transactions.

Features include:

* Record customer name
* Record borrowed amount
* Store transaction date
* Track payment status (paid or unpaid)
* Mark credit as settled
* View total outstanding credits

This reflects real sari-sari store practices where customers buy on credit.

---

# 7. Reports & Analytics

The system generates:

* Sales reports by date range
* Profit reports
* Inventory reports
* Credit reports
* Supplier-based product reports

Reports can be exported for record-keeping.
