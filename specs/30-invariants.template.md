# Invariants – TindahanPro

1. Product stock must never be negative.
2. Selling price must always be greater than or equal to buying price.
3. Every sale must reduce product stock.
4. Profit must always be computed correctly:
   Profit = Selling Price - Buying Price
5. A sale must always have a valid product and quantity.
6. Credit records must include a customer name and amount.
7. Credit status must only be "paid" or "unpaid".
8. Supplier must have a valid name and contact number.
9. All transactions must include a timestamp.
